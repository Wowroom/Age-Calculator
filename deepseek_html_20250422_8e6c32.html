About Our Age Calculator

Our free online age calculator tool helps you determine your exact age in years, months, days, hours, minutes, and seconds. Whether you're curious about your age down to the second or need to calculate someone else's age for official purposes, our precise calculator provides accurate results instantly.

How to Use the Age Calculator

Simply enter your birth date and time (or any date you want to calculate from) in the fields above, then click "Calculate Age". The tool will instantly display your exact age with a real-time counter that updates every second.
